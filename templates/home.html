Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Smart Invoice

How does the 60-second invoice creation work?

Our smart templates auto-save your business details and client information. Just select a client, add line items, and send. Taxes and totals calculate automatically. Most users create their first invoice in under 60 seconds!

Can I customize invoices with my brand?

Absolutely! Upload your logo, choose brand colors, customize email templates, and add business information. Every invoice will look professionally designed and on-brand.

What payment methods can I accept?

Smart Invoice integrates with Stripe, PayPal, and bank transfers. Clients can pay directly from the invoice with one click. You can also add custom payment instructions for checks or wire transfers.

How do payment reminders work?

Set automated reminders that send friendly notifications before and after due dates. Our system tracks opens and sends gentle nudges that maintain good client relationships while getting you paid faster.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We use bank-level encryption (256-bit SSL), secure cloud storage, and regular security audits. Your data is backed up daily. We're GDPR and SOC 2 compliant and never sell your information.
