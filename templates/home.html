Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know to get started

Is Smart Invoice really free?

Yes! Smart Invoice is completely free to use with unlimited invoices. No hidden fees, no credit card required, no trial periods. We also offer premium features for businesses that need advanced functionality, but the core invoicing platform is always free.

How does the 60-second invoice creation work?

Our smart form interface auto-saves your business info and client details. You just select the client, add items, and click send. Taxes, discounts, and totals are calculated automatically. Most users create their first invoice in under 60 seconds!

Can I customize invoices with my brand?

Absolutely! Upload your logo, choose brand colors, customize email templates, and add your business information. Every invoice you send will look professionally designed and perfectly on-brand.

How do payment reminders work?

Set automated reminders that send friendly payment notifications before and after the due date. Our system tracks opens and sends gentle nudges that maintain good client relationships while getting you paid faster.

What payment methods can I accept?

Smart Invoice integrates with popular payment gateways like Stripe, PayPal, and bank transfers. Your clients can pay directly from the invoice with one click. You can also add custom payment instructions for checks, wire transfers, or other methods.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We use bank-level encryption (256-bit SSL), secure cloud storage, and regular security audits. Your data is backed up daily, and we never sell or share your information with third parties. We're GDPR and SOC 2 compliant.
