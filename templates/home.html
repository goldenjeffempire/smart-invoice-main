Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Smart Invoice

How does the 60-second invoice creation work?

Smart Invoice uses AI-powered auto-fill that learns your business details and client information. Simply select a client, add line items, set payment terms, and send. Taxes and totals calculate automatically. Most users create their first invoice in under 60 seconds!

Can I customize invoices with my brand?

Absolutely! Upload your logo, choose brand colors, customize fonts and layouts, and set custom payment instructions. Every invoice will look professionally designed and perfectly on-brand. You can also create multiple templates for different purposes.

What payment methods can I accept?

Smart Invoice supports bank transfers, credit/debit cards via Stripe, PayPal, and custom payment instructions for checks or wire transfers. Each payment method can be enabled/disabled per invoice or globally.

How do payment reminders work?

Set automated reminders that send friendly notifications before and after due dates. Our system tracks opens, clicks, and sends gentle nudges that maintain good client relationships while getting you paid faster. You can customize reminder frequency and messaging.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We use bank-level encryption (256-bit SSL), secure cloud storage, and regular security audits. Your data is backed up daily and never sold. We're GDPR and SOC 2 compliant with enterprise-grade security infrastructure.

Can I manage team members?

Yes! Add unlimited team members with granular role-based permissions. Assign roles like Admin, Manager, or User. Set approval requirements, track all invoice activity in audit logs, and maintain full control over who can do what.
