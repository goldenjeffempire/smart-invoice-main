About Smart Invoice

We're on a mission to transform how businesses handle invoicing and payments

Our Story

Smart Invoice was born from frustration. Our founder, a freelancer, spent hours every month creating invoices in spreadsheets, chasing clients for payments, and manually tracking cash flow.

In 2020, we decided to build the invoicing solution we wished existed. Something simple, powerful, and designed for modern businesses.

Today, Smart Invoice helps 10,000+ businesses worldwide create professional invoices, get paid faster, and focus on what matters most: growing their business.
