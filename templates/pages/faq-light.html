FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about InvoiceFlow.

Getting Started

How do I create my first invoice?

After signing up, go to your dashboard and click "Create Invoice". Fill in your business details, add your client information, and include line items for your services or products. You can preview the invoice before sending it to your client.

How long does it take to set up my account?

Setting up your InvoiceFlow account takes just a few minutes. Sign up with your email, add your business information, and you're ready to create your first invoice. You can always update your business details later from the Settings page.

Can I import existing clients and invoices?

Yes! You can import client data using our CSV import feature. Simply prepare your data in the required format and upload it through the Import section. Our support team can also assist with data migration from other invoicing platforms.

Is there a mobile app available?

Our web application is fully responsive and works great on mobile devices. Native iOS and Android apps are currently in development and will be available soon. You can add InvoiceFlow to your home screen for quick access.

Billing & Payments

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), debit cards, and PayPal. For annual plans, we also offer bank transfer options. All payments are processed securely through our PCI-compliant payment partners.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time from your account settings. When upgrading, you'll only pay the prorated difference for the remaining billing period. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

What is your refund policy?

Monthly subscriptions can be cancelled anytime with no refund for partial months. Annual subscriptions include a 14-day money-back guarantee from the initial purchase date. After 14 days, you can cancel but no refund will be provided for the remaining period.

Features & Customization

Can I customize my invoice design?

Yes! You can add your company logo, customize colors, and save templates for future use. Go to Settings > Business to add your branding, or use our template system to create reusable invoice designs.

How do recurring invoices work?

Recurring invoices automatically generate on a schedule you set (weekly, monthly, quarterly, or yearly). Set up a recurring invoice once, and InvoiceFlow will create and optionally send new invoices to your client automatically.

What currencies are supported?

InvoiceFlow supports over 150 currencies worldwide. You can set different currencies for different clients and invoices. Currency symbols and formatting are automatically applied based on your selection.

Security & Privacy

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard AES-256 encryption for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. Our infrastructure includes firewalls, intrusion detection, and regular security audits. We're SOC 2 Type II compliant and follow GDPR requirements.

Do you offer two-factor authentication?

Yes! We support two-factor authentication (2FA) using authenticator apps like Google Authenticator or Authy. You can enable 2FA from your Security Settings for an extra layer of protection on your account.

Where is my data stored?

Your data is stored in secure, SOC 2 compliant data centers. We use encrypted databases and secure backup systems to ensure your data is protected and always available.
