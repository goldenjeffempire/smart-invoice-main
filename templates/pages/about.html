Our Story

InvoiceFlow was born in Lagos, Nigeria in 2021 from a simple frustration: why is invoicing so complicated? Our founder, Jeffery Emuodafevware, a software engineer and freelance consultant, spent countless hours wrestling with spreadsheets and clunky software just to send a simple invoice.

"I remember spending more time formatting invoices than actually doing the work I was billing for," Jeffery recalls. "I knew there had to be a better way."

What started as a side project to solve his own problem quickly gained traction among fellow freelancers and small business owners. Within six months, over 500 businesses were using the platform. Today, InvoiceFlow serves thousands of businesses across 45+ countries, processing millions in invoices every month.

We've bootstrapped our growth, focusing on sustainable development and listening closely to our users. Every feature we build starts with a real problem that a real business is facing. That's our promise: practical solutions that make your life easier.
