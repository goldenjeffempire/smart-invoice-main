Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your data

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly:

  • Account information (name, email, password)
  • Business information (business name, address)
  • Client information (names, email addresses)
  • Invoice data (line items, amounts, dates)
  • Usage data (features used, pages visited)

2. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Process and complete transactions
  • Send you technical notices and support messages
  • Respond to your comments and questions
  • Monitor and analyze usage trends
  • Prevent fraud and security issues

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share it only:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and prevent fraud
  • With service providers who assist us

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access using industry-standard encryption and security protocols.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and receive a copy of your personal data
  • Correct or update your personal data
  • Delete your personal data
  • Object to processing of your personal data
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time

6. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your user experience. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7. Changes to Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or through the service.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us.
