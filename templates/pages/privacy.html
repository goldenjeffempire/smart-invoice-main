Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 20, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account information (name, email address, password)
  • Business information (business name, address, contact details)
  • Client information (names, email addresses, payment details)
  • Invoice data (line items, amounts, dates)
  • Usage data (features used, pages visited)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process and complete transactions
  • Send you technical notices and support messages
  • Respond to your comments and questions
  • Monitor and analyze trends and usage
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud and security issues

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and prevent fraud
  • With service providers who assist in our operations

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect your personal information from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access. However, no Internet or email transmission is ever fully secure or error-free.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and receive a copy of your personal data
  • Correct or update your personal data
  • Delete your personal data
  • Object to processing of your personal data
  • Request restriction of processing your personal data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

6. Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our service and hold certain information. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@smartinvoice.com
