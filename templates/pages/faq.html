How do I create an invoice?

Sign up, click "Create Invoice," fill in your business details and client information, add line items with descriptions and amounts, and download your professional PDF. The entire process takes less than 2 minutes.

Is InvoiceFlow really free?

Yes! InvoiceFlow is completely free with unlimited invoices, PDF export, email/WhatsApp sending, custom branding, and analytics. No credit card required, no trial period, no hidden fees.

Do I need to install any software?

No installation required! InvoiceFlow is a cloud-based web application that works in any modern browser on desktop, tablet, or mobile. Access your invoices from anywhere with an internet connection.

Can I customize my invoices?

Absolutely! Upload your logo, set custom brand colors, add company details, include bank information, customize line items, and choose your currency. Every invoice reflects your unique brand identity.

What currencies are supported?

We support USD, EUR, GBP, NGN, CAD, and AUD with proper currency formatting and symbol placement. Select your preferred currency for each invoice to work with international clients.

How do I send invoices to clients?

Download the PDF and send it yourself, use our built-in email feature to send directly with the PDF attached, or share via WhatsApp with a pre-filled message. All options include the full invoice.

Can I track payment status?

Yes! Each invoice has a payment status (Paid/Unpaid/Pending) that you can update. Your dashboard displays real-time analytics showing total revenue, outstanding payments, and payment trends.

Can I edit invoices after creation?

Absolutely! Edit any invoice from your dashboard by clicking on it and making your changes. Updates are instant and you can regenerate the PDF with the new information.

Where is my data stored?

Your data is securely stored in the cloud with automatic backups. We use enterprise-grade infrastructure with 99.9% uptime to ensure your invoices are always accessible when you need them.

Can I delete my data?

Yes! You can delete individual invoices or your entire account at any time. Deleted data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.
