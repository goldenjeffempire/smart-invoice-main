Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an invoice?

Simply sign up for a free account, click "Create Invoice" from the dashboard, fill in your business and client details, add line items, and generate your professional PDF invoice instantly.

Is Smart Invoice free to use?

Yes! Smart Invoice is completely free to use. Create unlimited invoices, send them via email or WhatsApp, and track your revenue without any cost.

Can I customize my invoices?

Absolutely! You can upload your logo, set your brand color, add your business name, and include custom notes on every invoice.

What currencies are supported?

Smart Invoice supports USD, EUR, GBP, NGN, CAD, and AUD. You can select your preferred currency for each invoice.

How do I send invoices to clients?

You can download the PDF and send it manually, use our built-in email feature to send it directly with the PDF attached, or share via WhatsApp with a pre-filled message.

Can I track payment status?

Yes! Each invoice has a status (Paid/Unpaid) that you can update. The dashboard shows your analytics including paid invoices, unpaid invoices, and total revenue.

Is my data secure?

Yes, all data is securely stored in the cloud with industry-standard encryption. Your invoices and client information are private and only accessible to you.

Can I edit invoices after creation?

Yes, you can edit any invoice from your dashboard. Simply click on the invoice and select "Edit" to make changes.
