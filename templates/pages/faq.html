Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How do I create an invoice?

Sign up, click "Create Invoice," fill in your details, add line items, and download your PDF. It takes less than 2 minutes.

Is Smart Invoice free?

Yes! Smart Invoice is completely free. Create unlimited invoices, send them via email or WhatsApp, and track your analytics without any cost.

Can I customize invoices?

Absolutely. Upload your logo, set brand colors, add company details, include bank information, and customize line items.

What currencies are supported?

We support USD, EUR, GBP, NGN, CAD, and AUD. Select your preferred currency for each invoice.

How do I send invoices?

You can download the PDF, send via email with our built-in feature, or share via WhatsApp. All options include the full invoice attachment.

Can I track payment status?

Yes. Each invoice has a status (Paid/Unpaid) you can update. Your dashboard shows analytics with payment tracking.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted and securely stored in the cloud. Your invoices are private and only accessible to you.

Can I edit invoices after creation?

Yes. You can edit any invoice from your dashboard. Simply click the invoice and make your changes.
